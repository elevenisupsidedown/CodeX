About Us

StellarX is a non-profit organization committed to making astronomy accessible to everyone.
Our goal is to inspire curiosity and wonder about the universe by providing resources, insights, and events that bring people closer to the stars.

Events

Join us for stargazing nights, workshops, and talks led by experts in the field.
Our events are designed to engage enthusiasts of all levels, offering unique experiences to explore the universe together.

International Observe the Moon Night

Independence Square


14th October 2024

Celebrate the Moon with fellow sky-watchers around the world. Bring your telescope, binoculars, or just your eyes, and enjoy a night of lunar exploration.

Partial Solar Eclipse

Horton Plains


2nd November 2024

Watch as the Moon takes a bite out of the Sun. Join us for this stunning event and learn more about eclipses.

Hunter's Moon

Ritigala Mountain


17th October 2024

Enjoy the bright, orange glow of October’s full moon as it lights up the night sky.

Orionid Meteor Shower

Ella Observatory


21st October 2024

Lay back and enjoy the show as meteors from Halley’s Comet streak across the night sky.

Jupiter at Opposition

Pidurangala Rock


7th December 2024

Join us as Jupiter shines its brightest in the night sky. Perfect for telescope viewing!
